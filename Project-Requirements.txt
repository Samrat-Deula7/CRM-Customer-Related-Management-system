🔹 Detailed Features of OSOM CRM
1. Application & Document Management
Centralized storage of student applications, forms, and supporting documents.
Automated reminders for missing or expiring documents.
Secure sharing with students and partner institutions.

2. Visa Tracking & Compliance
Real‑time visa status updates for each student.
Alerts for deadlines, interviews, and renewals.
Compliance dashboards to reduce errors and delays.

3. Student Relationship Management
Complete student profiles (academic history, preferences, communication logs).
Personalized communication via email, SMS, or WhatsApp integration.
Automated follow‑ups to improve conversion rates.

4. Financial Tools
Fee tracking (application fees, tuition deposits, consultancy charges).
Automated invoices and receipts.
Integration with payment gateways for online transactions.

5. Workflow Automation
Task assignment and progress tracking for consultancy staff.
Automated notifications for next steps (e.g., “Visa interview scheduled”).
Reduced manual data entry through smart forms.

6. Analytics & Reporting
Dashboards showing student pipeline, conversion rates, and revenue.
Predictive analytics to identify high‑potential students.
Reports for management and compliance audits.

7. Collaboration Tools
Shared access for counselors, finance teams, and management.
Role‑based permissions to ensure data security.
Internal chat or notes for team coordination.

8. Scalability & Cloud Access
Cloud‑based system accessible from anywhere.
Mobile app support for counselors on the go.
Scalable infrastructure to handle growing student numbers.
